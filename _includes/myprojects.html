Robot Crowd Navigation by ConsideringInteraction and Occlusion

Demo
The proposed method comprehensively considers the influence of dynamic interactions among pedestrians and information occlusion on navigational decision-making. Our approach is trained using the PPO algorithm and validated in the "crowd_sim" simulation environment. Experimental results demonstrate that the proposed policy surpasses the performance of existing state-of-the-art methods.
